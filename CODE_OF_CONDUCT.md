# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Relix-Q OSS should be a respectful, useful place to collaborate on security
tooling. We expect participants to:

- Be kind, direct, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Keep discussions focused on the work and its impact.
- Avoid harassment, insults, discrimination, threats, or sexualized language.

## Scope

This code applies in project spaces including issues, pull requests,
discussions, docs, community chat, and project-related events.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainer team privately. For security
vulnerabilities, use [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, edit, or remove comments and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the community. Good-faith
mistakes should be corrected with clarity and proportion; repeated or malicious
behavior is handled more firmly.
